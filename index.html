About

Welcome to Soaps by Lucia! My name is Regina, and I started making soap about 10 years ago one of my children was allergic to many things, including store-bought soap. To ensure my child's safety and skin health were not harmed so I began experimenting with making soap. This journey led me to create high-quality, eco-friendly products that are gentle on the skin and are environmentally friendly.

My mission is to provide handcrafted soaps using only organic, fair trade, and sustainable ingredients.
